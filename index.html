Style Observations

  • Text: Sans-Serif type, specifically a Geometric Sans-Serif typeface. It appears similar to Helvetica or Helvetica Neue. The type varies in weight and size throughout the webpage.
  • Palette: #000000,#ffffff,#ffd4cd,#4f5bab
  • Imagery: There is a hero video at the top of the page, as you scroll down you'll see three images displayed in two columns. At the bottom of the page there are two more images advertising the company's other projects.
  • Header: The header is not fixed.The header consists of a navigation element, the website name, and the company's motto, as well as a caption.
